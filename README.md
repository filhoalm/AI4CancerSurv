# AI4CancerSurv
### CSU Hackathon on Cancer Surveillance and AI

The CSU/IARC Hackathon on Cancer Surveillance and AI brings together people interested in leveraging practical applications of AI for cancer surveillance. This is a collaborative, hands-on meeting and a free, informal discussion space to explore how artificial intelligence, agentic workflows, and modern data tools can advance cancer surveillance research and foster collaboration.

Each session features an invited presenter who shares a project, idea, or tool, followed by open discussion and brainstorming with participants. This is a collaborative, low-pressure environment focused on discussion and idea exchange rather than formal outcomes. Participants are welcome to simply listen, ask questions, and contribute ideas.
